About

This Electricity online calculator comes from the modernization program of Meralco, the objective of this program is to move our customer services online. We hope that this application will prove to be useful for our loyal customers in them, calculating their personal electricity usages and costs.

Directions on Using the Electricity Cost Calculator:

1. Type the Power Consumption, Hours of Use and Cost per Kilowatt Hour of your Preferred Appliance.

2. Click on Compute, to Know your Consumption and Cost per Day, Month and Year
