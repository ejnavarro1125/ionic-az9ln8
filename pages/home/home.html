Electricity Cost Calculator App

This Electricity Cost Calculator Application will automatically compute the amount of killowatts a certain applicance will use per Day, Month and Year by taking the amount of power it takes and multiply it by the amount of hours it is used. Then, the cost per day, month and year of the appliance will be known by using the power consumption of the product multiplied by the cost per kilowatt hour.

Directions:

1. Type the Power Consumption, Hours of Use and Cost per Kilowatt Hour of your Preferred Appliance.

2. Click on Compute, to Know your Consumption and Cost per Day, Month and Year
